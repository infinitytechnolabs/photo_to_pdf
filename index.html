Privacy Policy for Photo to PDF App

Effective Date: October 7, 2024

At Photo to PDF ("we," "our," or "us"), we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when using the "Photo to PDF" application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There is no login required, and we do not gather any personally identifiable data.

2. Permissions Used by the App

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera Access: To take photos for PDF creation.
  • Gallery Access: To select images from your device for PDF creation.
  • File Storage Access: To save the generated PDFs to your device and enable sharing or downloading.

3. Data Usage and Storage

All processing and PDF generation occur locally on your device. We do not send or store your images or PDFs on any external servers.

4. Password-Protected PDFs

If you choose to add a password to your PDF, both the password and encryption are handled on your device. We do not collect or have access to this information.

5. Your Control Over Data

Since all files are stored locally on your device, you can delete or share them as you choose. We do not access or manage your data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are encouraged to review it periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at infinitytechnolabs6@gmail.com.
